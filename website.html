Sorting Hat

Sorting Hat is a tool designed to quickly allocate hundreds of first-year students into their mandatory Exploration and Discovery (E&D) seminar courses. This project was developed over the course of a semester at Lewis & Clark College in Portland, Oregon, and my team consisted of Lars Mayrand, Nick Tan, Mack Beveridge, Sam Peers Nitzberg, and James Tostado.
